Text To Speech in Clearly

Sit back and let Clearly read blog posts, articles, and web pages to you thanks to the new Text-To-Speech feature, available exclusively for Evernote Premium subscribers.

How it works

Click the Text-To-Speech icon in the Clearly sidebar. Clearly will automatically detect the language of the article and read the text aloud.

Skip ahead

Only want to play back certain parts of an article? Use the sidebar buttons to skip to the next sentence, go back to a previous sentence, or pause playback.

Follow along

Clearly highlights the text as it reads so that you can follow along. You can continue using other Clearly features such as highlighting and clipping during playback.

Text To Speech in 21 languages

English, Japanese, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, Czech, Danish, Dutch, Finnish, Greek, Hungarian, Italian, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Swedish and Turkish.
